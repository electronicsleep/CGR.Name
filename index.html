# About

I enjoy music, programming, taking photographs, making websites and art, usually digital but getting back into sketching and painting. I collect classic video games and vintage synths. I started making music with a bass guitar and a drum machine around 1996.

Got going by making websites and programming around 1998 and making websites for my projects. I enjoyed it because I thought of it as a artistic medium like painting or drawing. You have a blank canvas with the web you can create anything and educate yourself, this I found inspiring and fun.

Have been fascinated by the command line too always learning new things with Unix operating systems. I believe in the Unix philosophy and that computers give us an ability to learn and educate ourselves if we choose it.
